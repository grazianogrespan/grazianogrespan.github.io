Unit✛Size Converter

A measure, currency, clothing conversion App! plus much more...





App Description

This free App lets you easily convert the main measures to their equivalent.

Its simple interface aim to be immediate and clear, just like it was at school. You can do equivalences of length, weight, volume, temperature and also you can do real-time conversions between the most traded currencies.

This is not only a units converter like others on the store!

Unit✛Size Converter can help you, for example, when you are abroad doing shopping and you don't know the exact size of your shoes. With its conversions of shoes,t-shirt,and trousers sizes you'll not have any fears of making mistakes.

SUPPORTED LANGUAGES : ENGLISH - ITALIAN.

App Features
  • Units conversions:
    • Length ➯ from pm to Mm.
    • Weight ➯ from fg to ton.
    • Volume ➯ from µl to m³.
    • Temperature ➯ Kelvin/Fahrenheit/Celsius.
  • Currency conversions:
    • Supported currencies:
      EUR/USD/GBP/AUD/CAD/JPY/
      CHF/SEK/HKD/NOK/NZD/MXN
  • Clothing conversions:
    All clothing conversions are divided in male and female section.
    • T-shirt
    • Shoes
    • Trousers
